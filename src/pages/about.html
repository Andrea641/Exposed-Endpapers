Case Study:

UX/UI Design for Youth Mental Health

Project Description

Do in-depth research on youth mental health issues and intervention methods used in the past. Go through the design process to sketch, create wireframes, journeys, flows, mockups and prototypes to create your own application to mitigate the issue.

Youth mental health can affect anyone, at any point in life and should be handled with the gravitas it requires. I will keep in mind as if I would release this to the public and design it accordingly. I must place emphasis that this application will provide links to real professional help through links and other contact methods.
