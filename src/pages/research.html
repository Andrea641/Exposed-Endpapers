Research

Study existing mental health platforms, apps, and community resources aimed at youth.

Identify what makes them successful or unsuccessful, both from a technical and emotional perspective.

Look at evidence-based therapeutic techniques such as cognitive behavioral tools, mindfulness practices, peer support systems, and crisis intervention pathways, and evaluate how these could be incorporated into an engaging user experience.

Use user research methods such as interviews, surveys, focus groups, or diary studies to understand young people’s daily routines, challenges, and aspirations.

Translate your findings into user personas, empathy maps, and experience journeys that will guide your design decisions.
